National Mentoring Program – Public Health Association Australia

In short, the program will pair mentors and mentees in public health. This will build capacity of student and early career members of the PHAA through teaching, training, networking, and providing them with appropriate resources.

See here for more information: https://www.phaa.net.au/about-us/mentoring

Environmental Health Public Health Officers COVID Response – Talent Pool

The Department of Health are seeking expressions of interest to register for our Environmental & Public Health COVID-19 response talent pool.

Register your application and be notified of temporary opportunities as they become available during our response to the COVID-19 pandemic.

See here for more information: https://www.seek.com.au/job/55760699?type=standout
